Works

20th may, 2019


Kochi



We went to 6 orphanages in kochi and we donated all our savings to those orphanages. We provided them with some basic knowledge of mathematics too!! It was indeed a heartwarming experience to see those little kids smile when they ate their food. CareFreeNow had finally set about its work and this marked the first of many journeys of ours.


16th August, 2019


Chennai



It was a famine struck area and we knew we had to do something about it. A lot fo services like providing food and clothes were provided and even medications and things were also given absolutely free of cost. Their eyes shone with greatfullness which we can never forget our entire lives


31st November, 2019


Mumbai



We went to one of the most densely populayed cities in the country and went from orphanage to orphanage providing free cleanup service and also some playing equipments for the kids to have fun.


18th January, 2020


Kolkata



This was a fun experience where we got to see lots of children happily running and playing. We gathered them around and told them stories, taught them some basic english and also played with the, Departing from them was sad but the experience was really worth it.
